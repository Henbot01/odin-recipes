SMASHBURGER

Truck Stop smashburger

A a great burger to take you back to back to those Road trips of yesteryear!

Ingredients

  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 250gm Diced Onions
  • 2 Slices of American Cheese
  • 2Tbsp tomato Sauce
  • 1 Pickled Gerkin
  • 2Tbsp of Mayonnaise
  • 1Tbsp of Hot English Mustard
  • 2 Brioche Buns
  • 2Tbsp Butter
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Method

  1. Very Lightly make four balls of mince the size of your hands
  2. Add butter to brioche buns and toast on a Flat Grill until golden brown
  3. Once toasted add the four beef balls in place of the buns and smash down with your spatula until desired thickness
  4. At the same time add the onions in pile near the meat
  5. Cook until the edges start to turn dark brown but the centre is still red
  6. When ready turn the patties and at the same time add the cooked onions to the top of two patties
  7. Top all four of the patties with a slice of American cheese
  8. whilst the cook prepare your buns with Sauce and pickles
  9. Once the patties are ready remove and stack on the buns
